Understanding the consequences of economic policy — before it’s passed.

Governments enact thousands of policies every year, mostly blind to what they will actually do. Lookback studies show existing methods of predicting policy effects perform worse than we’d hope for.

We’re building the first foundation model that forecasts a policy’s effects on prices, output, productivity, and the environment from its text and the market, cultural, political, and economic context it is proposed to be implemented in.
